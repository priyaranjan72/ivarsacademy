About Us

Institute of Vastu and Astro Research Studies (IVARS)

IVARS blends ancient wisdom with modern analytical approaches to provide practical, research-backed solutions in Vastu Shastra, Astrology, and Numerology. Our mission is to add value and impact lives through consultations, education and remedial programs that prioritise authenticity over superstition.

We emphasise a research-oriented methodology: every consultation and course is rooted in observation, measurement and proven principles. Our non-demolition remedies and tailored plans focus on wellbeing, harmony and measurable outcomes for homes, businesses and individuals.

Our team of trained consultants, researchers and faculty bring domain experience across KP Astrology, Vastu mapping, numerology and energy sciences. Through personalised guidance and structured learning, we help learners and clients achieve growth, balance and professional competence.
